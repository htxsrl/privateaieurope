Enterprise versions: are they enough?

ChatGPT Enterprise, Claude for Business and Gemini Advanced for Business offer additional guarantees:

  • DPA with European terms
  • No training on business data
  • SSO and user management
  • Audit logs of conversations

But concerns remain:

  • Data still passes through provider servers
  • Provider can change models unilaterally
  • No control over underlying infrastructure
  • Dependency on a non-European provider
  • Costs that grow linearly with user count

When to choose what

Choose ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini if:

  • You don’t process personal or sensitive data
  • You have few users (< 20)
  • You have no data sovereignty requirements
  • You want to start quickly without infrastructure

Choose ORCA (on-premise) if:

  • You process health, financial or personal data
  • You must strictly comply with GDPR and AI Act
  • You have 20+ users (lower total cost)
  • You want total control over models and data
  • You operate in regulated sectors

The open-source advantage

The open-source models used by ORCA — Llama 3, Mistral, DeepSeek R1, Qwen 3.5, GLM 5, Kimi 2.5 — in 2026 have reached comparable performance to proprietary models for most business tasks:

  • Document analysis and synthesis: equivalent performance
