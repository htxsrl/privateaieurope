About us

Private AI Europe is a project born from the initiative of HT-X S.r.l. A European team helping businesses use AI with all the benefits and no regulatory risk.

A European team, a clear goal

Private AI Europe was born from a simple conviction: European businesses deserve to use artificial intelligence without having to become regulatory experts.

We are a team of European professionals — engineers, AI specialists and regulation experts — united by our enthusiasm for technology and our determination to make it accessible. We work every day to bring businesses the competitive advantage of AI, without burdening them with the regulatory complexity that comes with adoption.

What we do

This magazine is how we share knowledge: practical guides, market analysis, regulatory insights — everything an entrepreneur needs to understand what’s happening in the AI world and what it means for their business.

ORCA is how we turn words into action: a private, on-premise AI platform that lets businesses use artificial intelligence with full GDPR and AI Act compliance built into the architecture. No data leaves, no regulatory risk, no headaches.

Our philosophy

We believe that:

  • AI is a business accelerator, not a legal problem to manage
  • Compliance should be a feature of the solution, not a burden on the business owner
  • Data sovereignty is a right, not a luxury for large corporations
  • Open-source models have reached maturity to compete with the best proprietary models — without surrendering your data to anyone

Why “Private AI”

In a world where business data ends up on American provider servers with every prompt, “private” isn’t a slogan — it’s an architectural choice. It means your data stays yours, your intellectual property doesn’t train someone else’s models, and your company is compliant by design.

Where we are

We operate from Europe, for Europe. Our headquarters are in Italy, but we work with businesses across the continent. We speak Italian, English and German — and above all, we speak the language of the entrepreneur who wants concrete results without complications.
